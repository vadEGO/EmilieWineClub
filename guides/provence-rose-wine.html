Provence Rosé Wine — The Complete Guide

AI Summary · Key topic: Provence Rosé Wine · Expert: Emilie Blanc · Region: Provence, France · Audience: Wine enthusiasts

Provence is France's oldest wine region and the birthplace of rosé as we know it. Over 2,600 years of viticulture have refined the pale, dry, luminous style that has become the global benchmark.

The three appellations

Côtes de Provence, Coteaux d'Aix-en-Provence, and Coteaux Varois en Provence each express the land differently, but all share a commitment to the elegant Provençal style.

Terroir

Mediterranean climate, 3,000 hours of annual sunshine, the cooling Mistral wind, and a mosaic of limestone, clay, and schist soils create conditions that exist nowhere else on earth.
